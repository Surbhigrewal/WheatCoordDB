About

WheatCoordDB converts coordinates from IWGSC CS RefSeq v2.1 to 24 hexaploid wheat assemblies using orthologous gene anchors projected by Liftoff.

Interpolation uses PCHIP splines fitted through ~95,000 gene anchor midpoints per assembly. Anchor density within ±5 Mb is reported as a confidence proxy — pericentromeric regions have fewer anchors and lower confidence.

Built by the Grewal lab, University of Nottingham.
